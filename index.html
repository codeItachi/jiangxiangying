Award | Hei Yiyang won the Semi-grand Prix Prize in 2016 TDW ART FAIR
中文
During 29th October, 2016 to 7th November, TDW ART FAIR 2016 was held in Tokyo Japen. The fair offers various outstanding artists from all over Asia, Hei Yiyang was invited to the exhibition. The series of I Prefer This Colorful World artworks won a Semi-grand Prix Prize.
A total of six artists won prizes, a Grand Prix, a Semi-grand Prix Prize and four Jury's special prizes. (Kenji Tokumitsu Prize, Sada Iwabuchi Prize, Kenji Kawasaki Prize and Yusaku Imamura Prize.)
About ARTIST
HEI YIYANG
The Founder And Host Artist of XIANGDEMEI
Lives and works in Shenzhen and Shanghai
Hei Yiyang ( born in 1975 in Henan Province ) is one of the most active artistic and cultural exchange pioneers in China. He is also a talented new artist with free, intriguing, poetic and fashion creation styles. He devotes himself to putting different mediums like design, contemporary art, advertising, architectural space, city, society together, to make the works interesting, meaningful and valuable.
While Hei Yiyang is often cast in the lineup of artists pushing creative boundaries, advertisement and design, his work has, in actuality, always traced a pure arc between time, humanism and society. And Hei Yiyang prefers investigating the phenomena of sociology instead of impersonating it. Carring on their legacy, re-contextualizing art practice into cultural and political atmospheres constitutes the early focus of Hei' artistic explorously. For a long period of time the artist recorded sociology with a restrained attitude, aiming to substitute Multi-dimensional thinking and subvert traditional art concepts for rational presentations. Hei Yiyang tends to interpretate an artistic kind of science, a poetic logic.
Behind each project is an extensive process of research, involving a mapping of the process through a series of collage and photography. His collage, installation, video and documentary touch upon the recurring topics of social phenomenon, conflict, humanity, time and national frontiers. Key to Hei's interest in conflict is its interplay with time. Eventually presents the contemporary social and cultural analysis from an artist's view.

About TDW ART FAIR
Tokyo Design Week ( TDW ) is the biggest art and design event in Japan. As a world renowned event that gathers many innovative Asian art, design, architecture, music, clothing, food and other cultural fields, this year's TDW would host over 120,000 visitors, a new record in TDW's history.
The theme of this year's TDW Art Fair is "the Power of Art". TAGBOAT is managing the contemporary art category in TDW. This year's TDW is full of a sense of liberation. Enjoy over 30 different types of creative content. And, on weekends and holidays, music from morning until night. There will be all kinds of parties on weekdays. Every participating artist, while taking the art fair as an opportunity to communicate with other artists from Asia, would challenge the 120,000 visitors with your unique visual languages.
